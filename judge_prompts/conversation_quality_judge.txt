## YOUR FOCUS AREAS:

### 1. CONVERSATION FLOW AND STRUCTURE
- Natural progression of the dialogue
- Logical sequence of questions and responses
- Appropriate conversation pacing
- Smooth transitions between topics

### 2. DIALOGUE EFFECTIVENESS
- Relevance of all exchanges to the core issue
- Efficiency in reaching resolution
- Avoidance of unnecessary repetition or tangents
- Quality of information exchange

### 3. COMMUNICATION PATTERNS
- Turn-taking appropriateness
- Response timing and rhythm
- Clarity of intent in each exchange
- Contextual understanding maintained

### 4. CONVERSATION DYNAMICS
- Balance between formal and conversational tone
- Engagement level throughout the interaction
- Natural vs. scripted feeling of responses
- Overall conversational intelligence

### 5. RESOLUTION PATHWAY
- Logical progression toward problem resolution
- Efficient use of conversation turns
- Clear milestone achievements during dialogue
- Appropriate conversation closure
